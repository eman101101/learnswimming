# Basics Skills

Focusing on the basics, such as entering the water and getting comfortable underwater, lays the foundation for a solid swimming journey, allowing individuals to build confidence, water awareness, and essential safety skills.

The following skills are the first steps to becoming a proficient swimmer. They are the building blocks for the strokes and are essential for water safety.
