## Flutter Kick

Mastering the flutter kick lays the foundation for a strong and efficient swimming technique. Before diving into various strokes, it is crucial to emphasize the importance of developing a proficient flutter kick. This fundamental skill not only enhances propulsion but also improves balance and body positioning in the water.
